Danish Crispy Pork recipe
Ingredients:
3-4 pounds pork belly with skin
1 tbsp coarse sea salt
1 tbsp coarse pepper
1 tbsp caraway seeds (optional)
2-3 cloves garlic, minced
2-3 bay leaves
1 onion, chopped

Instructions:
Preheat oven to 450°F (230°C).
Score pork skin, rub with salt, pepper, caraway seeds, and garlic.
Place pork in roasting pan, add bay leaves and onion, pour water around.
Roast at 450°F (230°C) for 30 mins, then reduce to 350°F (175°C) and roast for 1 1/2 to 2 hours until skin is crispy and internal temperature is 160°F (71°C).
Rest for 10-15 mins, then slice and serve.
