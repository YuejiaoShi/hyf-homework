Vietnamese Pho recipe:

Ingredients:
Beef bones, onion, ginger, star anise, cloves, cinnamon, cardamom, coriander seeds, salt, sugar
Rice noodles
Beef (sirloin, flank, or brisket), thinly sliced
Onion, green onions, cilantro, Thai basil, lime
Bean sprouts, hoisin sauce, sriracha

Instructions:
Boil beef bones, onion, and ginger for 10 minutes, then rinse.
Add spices and water to cover bones, simmer for 3-6 hours.
Cook rice noodles, slice beef and prepare toppings.
Strain broth, adjust seasoning.
Divide noodles into bowls, add beef and onion slices.
Pour hot broth over noodles and beef.
Serve with toppings and condiments.
